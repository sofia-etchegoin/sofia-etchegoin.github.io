Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?


EQ is having awareness about how you feel, and using that awareness to inform your decisions.


How is EQ different from IQ?


EQ refers to your ability to sense emotion in yourself and in others. IQ refers to your intellectual ability e.g. using logic to solve problems or understanding abstract ideas.
