Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity can help people:

  1. Embrace lifelong learning and personal growth.

  2. Recover from brain injuries.

  3. Break harmful habits and form positive ones.

  4. Improve their mental wellbeing.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit.

Through practise, practise, practise! Gotta fire off the JavaScript neural pathways many times to nail that lingo!


What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

I find that trying to explain what I’ve learnt is a great way of increasing my neuroplasticity. Especially if I happen to find a non-technical person who is good enough to not run away from techy conversation, it’s a good way of finding any gaps in my understanding. Likewise, if I put new information into different contexts, I’m bound to reinforce my understanding of it and therefore increase my neuroplasticity.


A resource that you found particularly useful or engaging.

You know what, ChatGPT is my go-to. If there’s a concept that I’m struggling to get my head around, I’ll interrogate chatGPT with a line of questioning until I understand it. It’s a great way of drilling new information without feeling like you’re wasting someone else’s time.


Growth Mindset


Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

A growth mindset is the belief system or attitude that capabilities can be developed over time through effort, learning and persistence. It’s relevant because it challenges the notion that intelligence or ability are static traits. Anyone can improve their abilities over time, and it’s empowering for people to know that.


In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

Nope! But it never hurts to reflect on these ideas every so often. It’s easy to forget about a growth mindset and the benefits of neuroplasticity when you’re in the middle of learning something difficult, hey.


How will you integrate ‘growth mindset’ into your learning journey?

By doing exactly that, I guess! Integrate that self-awareness of having a ‘growth mindset’ as part of completing the rest of this course.
